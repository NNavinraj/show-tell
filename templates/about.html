Pet Heaven

The primary objective of this project is to extend the use of image processing and annotation to mimic kids doing show-and-tell activities on a web-based platform. The usage of a machine show-and-tell in our case will be of use to both animal shelters and animal owners that seek to learn and identify common household animals in Singapore. This is in line with one common mission value held by SPCA and many other animal shelters, of which is to advance animal safety and well-being through general education and public awareness.
